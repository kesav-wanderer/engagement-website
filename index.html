Find Us

Venue & Directions

Bougainvillea Palace Villa

Luxury Event Villa
Address
Bougainvillea Palace Villa,
Old Mahabalipuram Road (OMR),
Muthukadu, Chennai — 603 112
Tamil Nadu, India
  • From Chennai City (via OMR):  Head south on OMR (IT Expressway) past Sholinganallur, Perungudi, and Siruseri. Continue until Muthukadu (~35 km from Anna Nagar, ~25 km from Tambaram).
  • From Chennai Airport:  Take GST Road to Pallavaram, connect to OMR via Radial Road. Approx 45–55 min depending on traffic.
  • From ECR (East Coast Road):  Take the Muthukadu junction and connect to OMR. The villa is near the Muthukadu Lake stretch.
  • Nearest Landmark:  Muthukadu Lake / ECR–OMR Junction, Muthukadu.
  • Parking:  No valet parking. Street parking outside the venue is available and free for all guests.
📍  Get Directions 📞  Call Venue

Note for guests: Please plan to arrive 10–15 minutes early. No valet parking — street parking outside is free. For assistance on the day, call  +91 86889 31666.
